Long Arm Services

We are pleased to have a long arm machine onsite and are able to offer long arm services in house.

We will work with you to finish your quilt into a work of art! You provide the top and the back. You choose the batting and thread. Then pick the perfect edge-to-edge design. We even have an option to add-on binding!
